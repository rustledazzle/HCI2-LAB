Character Classes

One handed sword

A versatile melee class that balances speed, defense, and offense. One-handed sword users can adapt to multiple playstyles, from quick solo combat to tanky frontline roles.

Katana

Masters of precise timing and counterattacks. Excels in high-risk, high-reward skill techniques. Katana users rely on timing and concentration for high burst damage and fluid combat flow.

Two Handed Sword

2HS users are heavy-hitting warriors that sacrifice speed for raw power. Their wide-reaching attacks and critical-based skills make them deadly on the battlefield.

Dual Wield or Dual Sword

Fast and flashy, dual sword users specialize in rapid combos and high mobility. They rely on precise timing and critical hits to overwhelm enemies.

Halberd

A burst damage class that uses skills with high critical potential. Halberd users are known for Dragon Tooth, a signature move that delivers massive damage with the right setup.

Knuckles

Knuckle users are close-combat specialists who emphasize speed, status effects, and combos. They are excellent for interrupting bosses and maintaining aggro with support options.

Magic Device

A flexible support and DPS hybrid class, ideal for AoE magic and crowd control. Magic Device users can buff allies, debuff enemies, and adapt to various party roles.

Staff

Staff users harness powerful magic to unleash devastating elemental spells. They are high-DPS casters, perfect for burst damage in both solo and party play.

Tank

A defensive build using a one-handed sword and shield to absorb damage and protect allies. OHS tanks control the battlefield with aggro management and defensive skills.

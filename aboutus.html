About Asobimo, Inc.

Asobimo, Inc. is a Tokyo-based game developer known for creating anime-style MMORPGs like Toram Online, Avabel Online, and Iruna Online. Founded in 2007, Asobimo focuses on immersive gameplay, character customization, and global multiplayer experiences for both mobile and PC players.
